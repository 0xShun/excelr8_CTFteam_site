About EXCELR8

ExcelR8 is a cybersecurity and tech research team founded by a group of friends from different institutions representing Region 8 in the Philippines. Founded in August 2024 to compete in the Hack4Gov 3 competition, our team has grown to bring together talented individuals from across the region. While our roots are in cybersecurity, our expertise extends to research, data science, and software engineering, making us a versatile force in tech innovation.

Our Mission

To recruit, train, and unite talented individuals passionate about cybersecurity and technology. Through our initiatives, we strive to:

  • Build a culture of collaboration in cybersecurity and tech research.
  • Advance knowledge and skills in data science, software engineering, and cybersecurity.
  • Increase cybersecurity awareness and resilience across Region 8.

What We’re Up To

  • CTFs: Think hacking challenges but in a competitive, fun way! We love testing our skills and learning through these events.
  • Research: Digging into cybersecurity problems and finding solutions that matter.
  • Projects: From apps to data analytics tools, we’re creating tech with real impact.
  • Sharing Knowledge: Whether it's cybersecurity, data science, or software engineering, we’re committed to educating and empowering others in the tech community.
